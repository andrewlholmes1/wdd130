California Sequoia National Park

Sequoia National park is a park in California where some of the largest trees in the world reside. This park has one of the largest trees on earth, the General Sherman Tree standing at 275 feet tall, a diameter of 25 feet, and a volume of 1,487 cubic meters. The tree gets it name from lieutenant James Wolverton in 1879 under the command of General Sherman. (Sources from Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia)
