Trips

We offer a variety of trips to suit all levels of experience and adventure. Whether you're a beginner looking for a gentle float down the river or an experienced rafter seeking the thrill of rapids, we have something for everyone. Our trips are designed to provide you with an unforgettable experience while ensuring your safety and enjoyment.

Our trips include half-day, full-day, and multi-day options, with varying levels of difficulty. We also offer specialized trips such as family-friendly excursions, corporate team-building events, and guided fishing trips. Each trip is led by our experienced guides who are passionate about sharing their love of the river and ensuring you have a great time.

To book a trip, please visit our Contact Us page or call us at (559) 123-4567. We look forward to welcoming you on the river!
